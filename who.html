An Overview of Teenwork.

TeenWork is an innovative youth employment program from CanAssist at the University of Victoria. The program is designed to help youth with disabilities find and retain meaningful, part-time paid employment while attending high school. TeenWork was developed with the aim of filling a gap in the employment field for youth with disabilities as they near transition to adulthood.

Each teen is carefully screened for work readiness and brings a unique skill set and the potential for growth to each workplace. Additionally, a TeenWork job coach provides non-time-limited, on-site job coaching, and supports every teen in working towards complete workplace independence.
